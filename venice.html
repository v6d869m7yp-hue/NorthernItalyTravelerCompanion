What matters most.

A compact reference for the decisions most likely to matter while you are there.

Best time outdoorsBefore 8:30 a.m. and again from late afternoon through blue hour.
Getting aroundWalk whenever practical; use the vaporetto for orientation, longer transfers and the experience itself.
Advance reservationsSt. Mark's Basilica, Doge's Palace and any special dinner that matters to you.
Good fallbackIf crowds become tiring, leave San Marco and shift to Dorsoduro or Cannaregio.
Departure mindsetDo not overfill the final morning. The drive into the Dolomites is part of the journey.

★★★★★
Allow
45–60 minutes with a timed entry.
Best for

Doge's Palace

★★★★★

★★★★☆
